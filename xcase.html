Circumstances of Disappearance

Randy Leach, who was a Linwood High School senior, was last seen at a pre-graduation party in rural Linwood, Kansas on April 16, 1988. By various accounts, he was too intoxicated to walk straight between that time and the last time he was reported seen, sometime between 01:15 and 02.00.

Leach was driving the family car and was reported missing by his parents on April 16 when he did not come home. Authorities do not believe he left of his own accord, since he left behind the 1966 Dodge Mustang he was restoring and his friends and family say he had no plans to leave home. The vehicle Leach had driven to the party has not been found, details of vehicle above.

In December 2002, Leach's case was reclassified from a missing person case to a homicide.
